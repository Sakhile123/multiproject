About Us


ABAGUDLI SECURITY & MULTI-PROJECTS was established in 2021 by Hendry Bhekinkosi Zulu. The company is a 100% black owned enterprise that strives to create job opportunities for as many people as possible and reduce poverty in the community at large. We believe that the youth is the future of tomorrow hence we try our level best to employ as many as possible.


OUR OBJECTIVES


  • To create employment opportunities.
  • To provide quality services to clients.
  • To maintain a relationship with our clients based on integrity, trust and mutual respect.
  • To provide value to our clients by reliability and accountability
  • To contribute towards the growth of the economy.
  • To provide necessary skills to the unskilled so that we provide quality services to our clients be it small or big.
    •


    OUR VISION


    Our Vision is to build a first-class security and multi-purpose company that will have active presence in south Africa and abroad.


    OUR MISSION


    Our Mission is to help our clients, the private sector and individuals in south Africa and abroad realize their dreams of safety and security, building structures and hygiene within their work environment.
